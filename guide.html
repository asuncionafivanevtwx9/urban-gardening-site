Starter Guide

  1. Find Light: 6–8 hours of sun is ideal. East/west windows are great.
  2. Pick Plants: Basil, mint, chives, lettuce—fast and forgiving.
  3. Choose Containers: 15–30 cm deep with drainage.
  4. Soil: Use lightweight potting mix, not garden soil.
  5. Water: Water deeply, then let the top 2–3 cm dry.
  6. Feed: A balanced liquid fertilizer monthly.
