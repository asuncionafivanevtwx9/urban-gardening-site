5 Easy Herbs for Apartment Growers

Reading time: 4 minutes

  1. Basil: Loves heat; pinch flowers to keep leaves tender.
  2. Mint: Pot alone to prevent takeover.
  3. Chives: Snip often—grows back fast.
  4. Parsley: Moist soil; harvest outer stems first.
  5. Cilantro: Prefers cooler temps; sow succession crops.
