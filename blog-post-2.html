Balcony Garden: Sun, Wind & Water

Reading time: 5 minutes

Balconies can be microclimates. Track sun for a week, note windy days, and group plants accordingly. Use heavier pots or secure railing planters to handle gusts, and water early mornings to reduce evaporation.
