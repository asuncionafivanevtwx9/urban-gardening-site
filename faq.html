Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a south‑facing window?

No. East or west exposure works for many herbs and leafy greens.

How often should I water?

When the top 2–3 cm of soil feels dry. Pots dry faster than ground soil.

Can I grow tomatoes indoors?

Yes, compact cherry varieties do well with strong sun and support.
