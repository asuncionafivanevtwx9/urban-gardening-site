Why Urban Gardening?

Healthy & Fresh

Pick your herbs minutes before cooking. Flavor and nutrients at their peak.

Small‑Space Friendly

Container setups fit on a balcony, fire escape, or sunny sill.

Low Cost

Start with seeds, a pot, and a bright spot—no yard required.

Quick Tips

  • Use pots with drainage holes.
  • Group plants by sunlight needs.
  • Feed monthly with balanced fertilizer.
  • Turn pots weekly for even growth.
